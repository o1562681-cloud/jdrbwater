Privacy Policy for JDRB Water Billing Effective Date: [may 20 2026] 1. Introduction JDRB Water Billing ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application. 2. Information Collection To provide our water billing and management services, the App collects the following types of information: • Personal Information: When you add a client, we store their Name, Address, and Contact Number. • Billing & Financial Data: We store water meter readings, billing periods, payment amounts, and payment history. • Account Information: We use Firebase Authentication to manage your account via your email and password. 3. Use of Permissions • Bluetooth: The App requires Bluetooth permissions (BLUETOOTH_SCAN and BLUETOOTH_CONNECT) solely to discover and connect to thermal receipt printers for printing bills and receipts. No location data is collected via Bluetooth. • Storage/Files: The App may access local storage to save receipt logos or export/import CSV backups of your data. • Biometrics: We use device-level biometric authentication (Fingerprint/Face Unlock) for the "App Lock" feature. We do not store or have access to your biometric data; it is handled securely by the Android System. 4. Data Synchronization and Third-Party Services The App uses Google Firebase (Firestore and Auth) to synchronize your data across devices. Your data is stored securely on Google’s servers. • We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or client data to third parties. • The App uses Google Play Billing for premium subscriptions. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. 5. Data Retention and Deletion • In-App Deletion: You can delete individual clients, bills, or payments at any time within the app. • Account Deletion: You can delete your entire account and all associated cloud data via the "Delete Account" button in the Settings menu. • Web-Based Request: If you cannot access the app and wish to request the deletion of your account and data, please email us at rexel50@gmail.com. We will process your request within 7 business days. 6. Security We implement industry-standard security measures, including data encryption in transit via HTTPS/SSL, to protect your information from unauthorized access. 7.

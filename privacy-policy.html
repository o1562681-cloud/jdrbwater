Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

JDRB Water Billing ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data for the JDRB Water Billing mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect client names, addresses, and contact numbers provided by the user to manage water billing records.
  • Financial Records: We store billing history, meter readings, and payment records.
  • Account Data: We use Firebase Authentication to manage user accounts via email.

2. Use of Permissions

  • Bluetooth: Used solely to discover and connect to thermal receipt printers. No location data is collected.
  • Biometrics: Used for the optional "App Lock" feature. We do not store or see your biometric data; it is managed by the Android system.
  • Notifications: Used to provide alerts regarding data synchronization and backups.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and synchronized to Google Firebase secure cloud servers. We implement SSL encryption for all data in transit.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Play Billing for premium subscriptions. We do not sell or share your personal data with any other third parties.
